Since 9th grade was online, I had a lot of freedom with how I did things. For example, I was able to work on things throughout the day without worrying about transit to and from school. However, group work was a pain. Many people refused to participate and it was hard to enjoy the work. Out of these classes, I enjoyed my concurrent enrollment classes for math, my LA class, Intro to Design, and AP Geo the most. I enjoy math and the logic that connects everything togther, so the challenging 1050-1060 math course was actually quite fun to work through with my friends. I also enjoyed my LA class becuase of the teacher; he taught us a lot about how to be concise and how to write better while also pushing us to view works in a different light. AP Geo was interesting because of the content; through this class, we looked further into things that are not really that deeply thought about. Finally, I love art so Intro to Design was a fun class.

To be honest, I didn't really enjoy this since I like writing the characters better; however, I can't deny it was a good way to improve my skill. I want to make sure I keep practicing.
