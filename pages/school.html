Since 9th grade was online, I had a lot of freedom with how I did things. For example, I had significantly more time to work on my homework and projects because of how some classes gave us free time, or how I didn't have to worry about the transit time from home to school. It was also nice being able to work on things throughout the day in such ease. However, group work was a pain. Many people refused to participate and it was hard to enjoy the work. Out of these classes, I enjoyed my concurrent enrollment classes for math, my LA class, Intro to Design, and AP Geo the most. I enjoy math and the logic that connects everything togther, so the challenging 1050-1060 math course was actually quite fun to work through with my friends. I also enjoyed my LA class becuase of the teacher; he taught us a lot about how to be concise and how to write better while also pushing us to view works in a different light. AP Geo was interesting because of the content; through this class, we looked further into things that are not really that deeply thought about. Finally, I enjoyed Intro to Design simply because I quite like art.
